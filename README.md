## Financial Modeling & M&A Analysis – Landmark Solutions Acquisition [Excel]

**Objective:**
Evaluated the potential acquisition of Landmark Solutions by Broadway Corp., a $120M transaction, through comprehensive financial modeling and valuation analysis.

Financial Models Used
* Pro Forma Financial Modeling: A 3-way financial model (Income Statement, Balance Sheet, Cash Flow) for both companies under Base and Pessimistic scenarios, incorporating historical data and operational driver projections (sales, working capital, PPE, financing).
* Cost of Capital Estimation: Calculated unlevered and levered betas using comparable companies, determined WACC for Landmark, Broadway, and the combined entity using CAPM, and recommended the appropriate discount rate for acquisition valuation.
* Capital Structure Analysis: Modeled merged pro forma financials under 100% debt and 50% debt financing scenarios, analyzing key credit metrics including solvency, liquidity, gearing, and debt service coverage.
* Valuation & Capital Budgeting: Performed DCF valuations for both companies pre- and post-acquisition, conducting sensitivity analyses on WACC vs. growth rate and WACC vs. operating margin to assess value drivers.
* Value Creation Assessment: Evaluated whether the $120M acquisition price was NPV-positive and analyzed the impact of debt vs. equity financing structures on shareholder value creation.

## Dynamic Amortization Calculator

**Objective:**
To provide a robust financial tool for evaluating long-term debt obligations, interest sensitivity, and accelerated repayment strategies.

**Purpose and Scope**
This model is designed to calculate monthly mortgage obligations and visualize the total cost of borrowing over the life of a loan. It incorporates dynamic inputs to allow for real-time scenario analysis of different interest rate environments and loan structure

**Key Features**
* Self-Adjusting Schedule: The amortisation schedule automatically grows or shrinks to perfectly match your chosen loan term, keeping the sheet clean and organized.
* Intelligent Input Guidance: Extra payment cells only highlight when they are relevant to your current loan length. This prevents entry errors and keeps you focused on active data.
* Protected Design: Complex financial formulas are locked to prevent accidental deletion, ensuring the calculator remains reliable while allowing you to easily tab through the necessary input fields.
* Instant Balance Updates: Your remaining balance updates immediately as you change interest rates or principal amounts, providing a live snapshot of your debt-reduction progress.
* Secure Data Entry: Restrictions are placed on specific cells to ensure you only enter valid data, protecting the integrity of the underlying financial model.
